Advent of Code 2024

It's December and time for this year's advent of code! I will be attempting to complete this coding challenge using Javascript, and building a simple site to showcase the results for each day.

Our challenge this year relates to the Chief Historian, who is missing. We need to look in 50 different places for him, and collect 50 stars by 25th December by completing the coding challenges. He will be in one of the 50 locations.

Day 1

Challenge 1

The first day begins! The Chief Historian was planning his trip before he disappeared, and has left location IDs of the various significant places he may visit. We have two similar lists of the location IDs, and must find the total distance between the list entries. We do this by finding the lowest entries in each list, finding the distance between them, then comparing the next two entries and so on. The distances between each entry are then added up to find the total distance between the two lists. I have converted the input data to JSON, stored in the data/ directory.
